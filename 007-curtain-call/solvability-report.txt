# Solvability Report: Curtain Call

## Verdict: SOLVABLE

## Dependency Chain Walkthrough:
1. Start in "The Grand Foyer" scene
2. Obtain "theater-key" and use it to solve "puzzle-1" to access "The Auditorium"
3. In "The Auditorium", obtain "ghost-light"
4. Use "ghost-light" to solve "puzzle-2" to access "Backstage"
5. Move to "Backstage" (cannot go up to catwalk due to broken ladder)
6. Move to "Dressing Rooms" and obtain "makeup-kit"
7. Return to "Backstage" and combine "ghost-light" with "makeup-kit" to create "illuminated-mirror" and solve "puzzle-3" to access "Catwalk"
8. In "Catwalk", obtain "cue-sheet" and "conductor-baton"
9. Use "cue-sheet" to solve "puzzle-4" to access "The Stage"
10. In "The Stage", use "illuminated-mirror" and "conductor-baton" to solve "puzzle-5"

## Win Condition:
Break the curse by using the illuminated mirror and conductor's baton on the ornate mirror upstage

## Orphaned Items:
None

## Unreachable Content:
None

## Specific Bugs Found:
None - all items and puzzles are logically connected with a clear progression path
