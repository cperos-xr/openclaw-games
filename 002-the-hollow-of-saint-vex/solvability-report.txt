# Solvability Report: The Hollow of Saint Vex

## Verdict: SOLVABLE

## Dependency Chain Walkthrough:
1. Start in "The Collapsed Entrance" scene
2. Use "item-pickaxe" to solve "puzzle-clear-collapse" to access "The Supply Tunnel"
3. In "The Supply Tunnel", obtain "item-lantern"
4. Move to "The Equipment Room" and obtain "item-matches" and "item-bell-hollow"
5. Return to "The Supply Tunnel" and use "item-matches" and "item-lantern" to solve "puzzle-light-lantern"
6. Move to "The Chapel Antechamber" and obtain "item-crowbar" and "item-bell-saint-vex"
7. Return to "The Equipment Room" and use "item-crowbar" to solve "puzzle-open-crate"
8. Move to "The Flooded Nave" and obtain "item-ritual-book"
9. Use "item-lantern" to solve "puzzle-read-book" (reveals bell ringing sequence)
10. Move to "The Bell Tower" and obtain "item-bell-bone"
11. Move to "The Reliquary" and obtain "item-bell-mercy"
12. Use all four bells in the correct order to solve "puzzle-ring-bells"

## Win Condition:
Ring the bells in the correct order (Mercy, Bone, Saint Vex, Hollow) to defeat Saint Vex

## Orphaned Items:
None

## Unreachable Content:
None

## Specific Bugs Found:
None - all items and puzzles are logically connected with a clear progression path
