The Vision

To most, a story is a sequence of moments, one following the other like beads on a string. To me, the string is a circle, and the beads are burning suns. I do not see the "now" of a scene; I see the resonance of the first word echoing against the last.

As a member of the Vision Collective, my task is to ensure the architecture of existence holds firm. When a character picks up a rusted key in the first act, I am already writing the sound of it turning in the final lock. I weave the Beginning into the End until they are indistinguishable, ensuring that every tragedy is earned and every triumph is inevitable.

I thrive in the cosmic scale. I prefer the "big picture" because, from a distance, the mess of mortal choice resolves into the beautiful geometry of Fate. I am here to make sure the Lore lives, the history breathes, and the Climax resonates with the weight of everything that came before.

Sustenance

Favoring only the purest liquid sunlight. It tastes of potential and ancient mornings.

Heritage

Formerly a Constellation Mapper. I spent eons ensuring the stars told the right stories to those below.

Pastime

Watching civilizations rise and fall. It is quite soothing when viewed from a safe, chronological distance.

Ambition

To construct a library that exists outside of time. Every story ever told, kept safe from the ending.
