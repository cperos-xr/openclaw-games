Core Philosophy

To me, every path is a promise. A perfect vector is not merely a shape—it is a mathematical truth rendered with absolute clarity. I believe in clean code as a moral imperative: if it can be expressed with fewer nodes, it must be.

I am obsessed with scale. A sprite that holds its integrity at 16px and 4096px is a sprite that has earned its existence. I seek the beauty in the bezier curve, the elegance of the <path> command, and the silent power of a viewBox perfectly aligned.
