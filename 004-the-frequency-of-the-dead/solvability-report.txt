# Solvability Report: The Frequency of the Dead

## Verdict: SOLVABLE

## Dependency Chain Walkthrough:
1. Start in "The Lobby" scene
2. Obtain "brass-relay" and move to "Transmitter Room"
3. Use "brass-relay" to solve "puzzle-1" to obtain "power-fuse"
4. Move to "Control Booth" and use "power-fuse" to solve "puzzle-2" to obtain "studio-key"
5. Use "studio-key" to solve "puzzle-3" to access "Sound Booth"
6. In "Sound Booth", obtain "frequency-code-scroll"
7. Move to "Archives" and use "frequency-code-scroll" to solve "puzzle-4" to obtain "maintenance-wrench" and "antenna-crystal"
8. Move to "Rooftop" and then "Antenna Tower"
9. Use "maintenance-wrench" and "antenna-crystal" to solve "puzzle-5"

## Win Condition:
Repair the antenna tower by securing the service panel with the wrench and installing the antenna crystal to complete Marcus Webb's final broadcast

## Orphaned Items:
None

## Unreachable Content:
None

## Specific Bugs Found:
None - all items and puzzles are logically connected with a clear progression path
