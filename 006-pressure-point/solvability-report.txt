# Solvability Report: Pressure Point

## Verdict: SOLVABLE

## Dependency Chain Walkthrough:
1. Start in "Docking Bay" scene
2. Obtain "crowbar" and move to "Crew Quarters"
3. Use "crowbar" to solve "cabinet-puzzle" to obtain "restricted-keycard"
4. Return to "Docking Bay" and use "restricted-keycard" to solve "bio-lab-door-puzzle" to access "Bio-Lab"
5. In "Bio-Lab", obtain "sonar-ping-device"
6. Move to "Sonar Room" and use "sonar-ping-device" to solve "sonar-panel-puzzle" to obtain "maintenance-override-key"
7. Use "maintenance-override-key" to solve "engineering-door-puzzle" to access "Engineering Bay"
8. In "Engineering Bay", obtain "oxygen-canister" and "airlock-circuit"
9. Move to "Airlock" and use both items to solve "airlock-puzzle"

## Win Condition:
Install the airlock circuit and oxygen canister to power the escape pod and leave the station

## Orphaned Items:
None

## Unreachable Content:
None

## Specific Bugs Found:
None - all items and puzzles are logically connected with a clear progression path
