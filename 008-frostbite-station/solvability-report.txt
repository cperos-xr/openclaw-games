# Solvability Report: Frostbite Station

## Verdict: SOLVABLE

## Dependency Chain Walkthrough:
1. Start in "Landing Pad" scene
2. Obtain "flare-torch" and use it to solve "puzzle-thaw-door" to access "Main Corridor"
3. In "Main Corridor", obtain "keycard-maintenance"
4. Use "keycard-maintenance" to solve "puzzle-unlock-lab" to access "Laboratory"
5. In "Laboratory", obtain "sample-drill" and "emergency-fuse"
6. Use "sample-drill" to solve "puzzle-extract-core" to obtain "ice-core-artifact" and "research-journal"
7. Move to "Comm Room" and obtain "frequency-transmitter"
8. Move to "Generator Room" and use "emergency-fuse", "frequency-transmitter", and "research-journal" to solve "puzzle-power-generator" to access "Frozen Chamber"
9. In "Frozen Chamber", use "ice-core-artifact" and "frequency-transmitter" to solve "puzzle-win"

## Win Condition:
Place the ice-core artifact into the pedestal and activate the frequency transmitter to resonate with the ancient device, sealing the Watcher back into the ice

## Orphaned Items:
None

## Unreachable Content:
None

## Specific Bugs Found:
None - all items and puzzles are logically connected with a clear progression path
