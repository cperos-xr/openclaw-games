# Solvability Report: The Ashmore Sanatorium

## Verdict: SOLVABLE

## Dependency Chain Walkthrough:
1. Start in "The Foyer" scene
2. Obtain "master-key" and use it to solve "unlock-records" puzzle to access "Records Room"
3. In "Records Room", obtain "cipher-wheel"
4. Use "cipher-wheel" to solve "decode-cipher" puzzle to reveal location of "battery-cell"
5. Move to "Electrotherapy Chamber" and obtain "battery-cell"
6. Use "battery-cell" to solve "power-generator" puzzle to access "Ice Cellar"
7. In "Ice Cellar", obtain "patient-ledger"
8. Use "patient-ledger" to solve "open-safe" puzzle to obtain "resonance-key"
9. Move to "Director's Office" and obtain "resonance-key"
10. Use "resonance-key" to solve "unlock-observatory" puzzle in the "Observatory" scene

## Win Condition:
Insert the resonance key into the Frostwell's control panel to seal the supernatural cold

## Orphaned Items:
None

## Unreachable Content:
None

## Specific Bugs Found:
None - all items and puzzles are logically connected with a clear progression path
