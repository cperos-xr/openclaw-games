# Solvability Report: The Clockwork Lighthouse

## Verdict: SOLVABLE

## Dependency Chain Walkthrough:
1. Start in "Lighthouse Exterior" scene
2. Move to "Ground Floor Workshop" and solve "workshop-puzzle" to obtain "brass-key"
3. Use "brass-key" to solve "library-puzzle" in the "Library & Observatory" scene to reveal "gear-sequence"
4. In "Gear Chamber", use both "chronometer" and "gear-sequence" to solve "gear-chamber-puzzle"
5. Move to "Living Quarters" to find "lens-crystal"
6. In "Lighthouse Lantern Room", use "lens-crystal" to solve "beacon-puzzle"

## Win Condition:
Install the lens crystal in the beacon to break the time loop

## Orphaned Items:
None

## Unreachable Content:
None

## Specific Bugs Found:
None - all items and puzzles are logically connected
